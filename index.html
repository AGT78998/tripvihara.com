Pawapuri

A famous Jain pilgrimage site in Bihar, known as the place where Lord Mahavira attained nirvana. It is famous for the beautiful Jal Mandir, a marble temple situated in the middle of a lotus pond. Pawapuri attracts thousands of pilgrims and tourists every year because of its spiritual and historical importance.

Rajgir

A historic city in Bihar known for its religious and cultural importance. It was the ancient capital of the Magadha kingdom and is associated with both Buddhism and Jainism. Rajgir is famous for attractions like the Vishwa Shanti Stupa, hot springs, and beautiful hills surrounding the city.

Bodh Gaya

The place where Gautama Buddha attained enlightenment beneath the sacred Bodhi Tree — one of the most spiritually significant sites in the world, drawing pilgrims from every corner of the globe.

Nalanda

Nalanda is famous for the ancient Nalanda University, one of the world’s oldest centers of learning. It was a major Buddhist educational center that attracted students from many countries. Today, the ruins of Nalanda are an important historical and tourist attraction in India.
