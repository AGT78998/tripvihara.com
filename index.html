BodhGaya

Bodh Gaya is one of the most sacred spiritual sites in the world and an incredibly important part of the cultural identity of Buddhism and its followers. It is the place where Gautama Buddha attained enlightenment under the sacred Bodhi Tree over 2,500 years ago. Located in Bihar, the town is home to the historic Mahabodhi Temple, one of the most important pilgrimage sites in the world. Despite its deep spiritual importance, the surrounding area offers peaceful monasteries, cultural experiences, local cuisine, and serene views that attract pilgrims and travelers from across the globe.
